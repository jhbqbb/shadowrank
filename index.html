01 / Who We Are

Every warrior starts at E Rank. No shortcuts. No cheat codes. Just 60 days of precision training that levels your body and earns your rank. We built the fitness system that treats your progress like the game it was always meant to be.

02 / The System
Daily Quests.

Wake up to a new mission every morning. Complete the exercises, earn XP, and watch your rank rise. Miss a day and the streak resets. No mercy.

Guided Workouts.

Step-by-step exercise sessions with 3D form cues and live rep counters. From cable rows to bodyweight circuits — every movement tracked, every set counted.

Run Tracker.

GPS-powered runs that count toward your daily quest. Distance, pace, duration — every kilometre feeds your XP total. Real movement. Real rank progression.

Guild Wars.

Join a guild, compete on weekly leaderboards, and rise together or fall alone. Invite codes. Private guilds. Public discover. The strongest team wins the week.
