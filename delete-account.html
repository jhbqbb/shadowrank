1. In-App Deletion

You can delete your S Rank account directly inside the app:

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the deletion prompt.

This permanently removes your account from active systems and signs you out of the app.

2. If You Cannot Access The App

Email supportsrank@gmail.com with the subject line Delete S Rank Account. Send the request from the email address attached to the account, or include your friend code and any other details needed to identify the account.

3. Data Deleted

When an account deletion request is completed, we delete or remove access to the following items from active systems where applicable:

  • Firebase Authentication account
  • Public profile and private progression data
  • Avatar image stored for the account
  • Device notification tokens tied to the account
  • Pending friend-request records and guild membership links owned by the account

Subscription billing records remain with Google Play and are governed by Google's billing terms. Some backup copies may persist for up to 30 days before final removal.

4. Health Connect

Health Connect data is read on-device. Deleting your S Rank account does not remove the source data stored in Health Connect itself. To revoke access, open Health Connect settings on your Android device and remove S Rank access there.

5. Contact

Questions about account deletion can be sent to supportsrank@gmail.com.
