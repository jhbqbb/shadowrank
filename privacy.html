1. OVERVIEW

S Rank ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the S Rank mobile application ("App") or visit shadowrank.app.

By using the App or Website, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Policy. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

2. DATA WE COLLECT

We collect the following categories of data:

CategoryExamplesPurpose
Account DataEmail address, display name, profile photo (from Google)Create and manage your account
Profile DataUsername, age, height, weight, fitness level, goalsPersonalise workouts and AI quests
Fitness DataWorkouts completed, XP, level, streak days, quest historyTrack your progress
Health DataSteps, heart rate (via Health Connect, optional)Provide health-based insights
Device DataDevice type, OS version, app versionFix bugs and improve stability
Usage DataScreens viewed, features used, crash reportsImprove the App experience
Community DataGuild membership, chat messages, leaderboard rankingPower community features

We do not collect precise geolocation, payment card details (managed by app stores), or any biometric data beyond optional health integrations you explicitly enable.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account and profile.
  • Generate AI-powered daily workout quests tailored to your goals and equipment.
  • Track your workout history, XP, levels, and streaks.
  • Power community features such as guilds and the global leaderboard.
  • Send push notifications about quests, achievements, and updates (you can disable these).
  • Diagnose and fix bugs through crash reporting.
  • Improve the App by analysing anonymised usage patterns.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not use your data for targeted advertising or sell it to advertisers.

4. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

S Rank uses the following third-party services. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Storage) — account management, data storage, and AI quest generation. Firebase Privacy
  • Google Sign-In — authentication provider. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI — generates personalised workout quests via Cloud Functions (your profile data is sent to generate quests, not stored by Google for training). Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy
  • Sentry — crash and error reporting (anonymised). Sentry Privacy
  • Google Health Connect — optional health data integration (steps, heart rate). Data stays on your device unless you explicitly share it.
  • Google Mobile Ads — non-targeted ads shown to free-tier users. Premium users see no ads.

5. DATA STORAGE & SECURITY

Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Storage), with servers primarily located in the European Union. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Firebase security rules that restrict data access to authenticated users only.
  • Server-side validation to prevent data manipulation.
  • Rate limiting on sensitive operations (e.g., XP updates).

No method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

6. DATA RETENTION

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account:

  • Your profile and fitness data will be deleted from our systems within 30 days.
  • Anonymised aggregate data (e.g., total workouts completed globally) may be retained.
  • Chat messages in guilds may persist for up to 90 days before deletion.
  • Crash reports in Sentry are automatically deleted after 90 days.

Backup copies may persist for up to 30 additional days after deletion. Legal obligations may require us to retain certain data longer.

7. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights under applicable data protection law (including GDPR):

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Restriction: Request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@shadowrank.app. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

S Rank is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will delete that information promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@shadowrank.app.

9. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

S Rank operates globally. Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence, including the United States, where Google's infrastructure is based. These transfers are made subject to appropriate safeguards, including Google's adherence to EU-US Data Privacy Framework and Standard Contractual Clauses.

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will notify you through the App or via email where practicable.

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Policy.
