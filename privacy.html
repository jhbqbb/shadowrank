1. Overview

S Rank ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. By using the S Rank app ("the App"), you agree to the practices described here.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Data: Email address and password hash when you create an account.
  • Profile Data: Display name, avatar image, age, weight, height, fitness goals, and equipment preferences you provide during onboarding or in settings.
  • Fitness Activity: Daily quest completions, XP earned, rank progression, streak days, workout sessions, and training program progress.
  • Device Data: Device type, operating system, and app version, used for crash reporting and compatibility.
  • Purchase Data: Subscription status and purchase history, managed through Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not store your payment card details.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and personalize your fitness experience (quests, training plans, rank system)
  • Sync your progress across devices
  • Display leaderboard and guild features
  • Send account-related emails (password reset, important notices)
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Process and manage subscriptions

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Auth), hosted in Google Cloud. We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (TLS), access controls, and Firestore security rules to protect your information.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase — Authentication, database, and storage
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and purchase management
  • Google Play / Apple App Store — App distribution and in-app purchases
  • OpenAI — AI-generated training program content (no personal fitness history is shared beyond your goals and rank level)

6. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us at supportsrank@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability (receive your data in a structured format)

To exercise these rights, contact us at supportsrank@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
